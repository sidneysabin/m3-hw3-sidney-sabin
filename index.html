About Gryffin

Dan Griffith "Gryffin" is a DJ, songwriter, and record producer. He is a San Francisco native and is Japanese-American. While studying electrical engineer at the University of South California, he spent much of his leisure time on music. The name "Griffin" is a combination of his last name, Griffith, and the mythical bird griffin.
